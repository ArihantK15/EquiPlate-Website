How We Create Impact

Notification: Partner hostels notify us of surplus by 9 PM.
Quality Check: Volunteers verify freshness & hygiene.
Rapid Transport: Fleet collects and transports within 45 min.
Distribution: Food reaches shelters before 10:30 PM.

Impact Stories

To the Receiver – The Shelter
"Budget cuts made it hard to provide nutritious dinner every single night. EquiPlate didn’t just bring food; they brought variety. One night it's paneer, the next it's mixed veg. The smiles on the residents' faces when the EquiPlate van pulls up are priceless." — Sister Anita, Caretaker, Hope Foundation Old Age Home

From the Source – The Student Hostel
"We used to throw away nearly 10kg of rice and dal every night simply because attendance fluctuated. Seeing that food go to the children at the Sunshine Orphanage instead of the trash bin has changed the culture of our hostel. Our students are now more mindful, and our waste is purposeful." — Ramesh V., Mess Manager
